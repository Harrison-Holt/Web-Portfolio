Welcome to the Web of History!

Step into the past and explore the interconnected events that shaped our world. This timeline provides a guided journey through pivotal moments in history, starting with the Seven Years' War and progressing through the American Revolution, French Revolution, Napoleonic Wars, and finally, the War of 1812.

Each timeline is designed to give you a detailed understanding of how these events influenced one another, with key dates, major figures, and significant outcomes highlighted along the way. Navigate through history at your own pace using our accessible and interactive features.

What to Expect:

  • Detailed Timelines: Dive into the events of each era, complete with chronological markers and detailed descriptions.
  • Seamless Navigation: Use the 'Next' button to move forward through history, or revisit earlier events with ease.
  • Accessible Design: This app is built with accessibility in mind, offering keyboard navigation, high-contrast mode, and screen reader support.
